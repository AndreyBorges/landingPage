About

The main objective of the architect is to plan, project, and design urban spaces to improve the quality of life of the people who live there. people who live in them. To do this, he takes into account the technical, historical cultural, and aesthetic aspects of the environment.
The architect's profession is regulated. To exercise it, it is mandatory to have a bachelor's degree in Architecture in Architecture issued by a higher education course recognized by the MEC and to obtain professional professional registration with the Architectural and Urban Planning Council of the region where where he/she works.
